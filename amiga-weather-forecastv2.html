📖 System Information
▼

The SAGER WEATHERCASTER is a precision meteorological instrument that has been in continuous use since 1942. This scientific tool provides accurate weather forecasts for a 30-mile (50km) radius based on five thousand variations of input data.


Originally developed for the United States Air Force, this algorithm embodies over 35 years of forecasting experience. The system generates 12-24 hour forecasts for any location north of 25° latitude.


For maximum accuracy, observations should be made at frequent intervals during rapidly changing conditions.
